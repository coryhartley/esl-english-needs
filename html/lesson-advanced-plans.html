Teaching Advanced

Free class activities and resources

Everything you need to make sure you make or choose the right lesson plan. Here you can find a wide range of practical resources to use in your classroom, including free worksheets class plans, exam practice and video activities, all designed to engage and challenge more mature learners

C1 Report Writing - Woodstock 99

This lesson will cover how to write a report including the basic structure, objective and useful expressions. Once familiar, the students will use a short documentary about the Woodstock 99 disaster concert to put what they have learned into practice. Rock on!

C1 Inversions - A Weekend Off

This is a grammar lesson focusing on inversions based around the topic of the stresses of parenthood. Students read a text about new parents who get a weekend off, then transform the text using inversions. Download the handout and teacher’s notes below.

Advanced Conditionals

Below are two comprehensive presentations explaining and practicing advanced conditional forms. These include mixed and inverted and how to use them to your benefit in the exam with example tasks. Essential for any C1 conditionals class.

C1 Conditionals - Lottery

Teach the advanced conditional forms with this lesson plan and worksheet. Practice using inversions and mixed form to this news article about winning the lottery (or not winning).
