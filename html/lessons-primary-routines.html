Routines and Frequency- Present Simple

Every student must master the Present Simple. After this lesson, they will have no problems speaking and writing about the present.

About this lesson plan

Cover the basics and make sure your students are 100% comfortable with the Present Simple. When do we use it? How can we use Adverbs of Frequency to improve speaking about the present? After this lesson, your students will wonder no more. At the end, there is a short writing task to check their understanding.

Aims:

  • To understand the Present Simple tense and how it is used to speak about habits and routines
  • To learn and correctly use Adverbs of Frequency to enhance writing and speaking about routines

Age and level:

Primary learners at A2+/B1 level

Time:

60 minutes
