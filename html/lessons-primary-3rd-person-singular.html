3rd Person Singular - Present Simple

Use this lesson plan to practice that tricky 3rd person singular verb with a fun and interactive project

About this lesson plan

Are your students continually forgetting to use the correct verb form for he/she/it during their speaking and writing? The 3rd person singular can be a tricky one to master, especially regarding when to use 's', 'es' or 'ies' endings to the verb.

Aims:

  • To learn how verbs are formed in the 3rd person singular tense
  • To build confidence in using these verb forms in writing and speaking
  • To create an imaginative storyboard incorporating the target grammar

Age and level:

Primary learners at A2+/B1 level

Time:

90 minutes
