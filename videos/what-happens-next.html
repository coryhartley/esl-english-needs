What Happens Next?

Have fun with your students watching this compilation video where we pause right before the action and we ask: "What happens next??..."

As well as enjoying the silly video clips, your students will be given the opportunity to speculate and make sentences based on what they think will happen next. Points and praise can be given to whoever has the closest prediction, or whoever has the most grammatically correct sentence.

They can complete their predictions in the present tense (with the 3rd person singluar) or in the past tense, depending on their level. Enjoy!
