Lion King - Animal Search

Disney is always a sure-fire hit to get your young learners' attention. Disney's 'Lion King' is the perfect way to test your students knowledge of animals while introducing some less common animal and nature vocabulary.

Why not transport your students to Africa to learn all about the weather, fascinating habitats and creatures that roam the savannah.

As a bonus, they can try their hand at drawing Simba with an extremely simple 'how to draw' video.
